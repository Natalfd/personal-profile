My Hobbies.

Sewing

I am a self-taught sewist, making my way through a catalog of patterns and fabrics.

I love sewing garments with linen, silk and wool. My favorite things to make are coats and jeans, though I often find a good, quick basic is equally satisfying.

Plants

My vast collection includes many tropicals, cacti and succulents. My current favorites are the velvet-leaf Anthuriums.

I plan on building an indoor greenhouse to provide the tropical plants a proper growing environment.
